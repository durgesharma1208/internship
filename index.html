Have Questions?

Find answers to the most common questions about our services.

How do I book a train ticket?

Simply enter your source and destination, choose your date, select a train from the list, provide passenger details, and complete the payment. It's that easy!

What are the payment options available?

We accept all major payment methods including Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.), and various mobile wallets.

Can I get a refund on cancellation?

Yes, refunds are processed instantly as per policies. The amount will be credited back to your original payment method.

Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

Yes, you can modify details like travel date or passenger information within the allowed time frame, depending on the train's booking rules.

Is my personal information safe?

Absolutely! We use secure encryption protocols and comply with data protection laws to keep your personal and payment information safe.

Are there any discounts available?

Yes, we frequently offer special promotions and discounts. Keep an eye on our website or subscribe to our newsletter for the latest offers.

What should I do if I face a payment issue?

If your payment fails or is delayed, please check your payment method, ensure sufficient funds, and try again. You can also contact our support team for assistance.
